About Us

A Tradition of Excellence Since 2006

Who We Are

Pearl Packaging Company

Pearl Packaging Company is a corrugated packaging manufacturer based in Karachi, Pakistan. Established in 2006 the privately owned company provides a first class service to the packaging industry. The cardboard manufacturer is one of the biggest independent unit in Karachi and continues to grow in the packaging field. Along with its sister companies, Pearl Packaging Company delivers packaging solutions from design, to manufacturing and printing.

At Pearl Packaging we offer a comprehensive range of cardboard products, from a basic carton box to the most intricate and unique die-cut boxes. We specialize in providing the ideal packaging solutions for individual customers and deliver an exceptional customer service.

Why People Like Us?

Competitive & Reliable

It's simple. We like to quote competitive prices, establish long term customer relationships and deliver the products on time, every time. Our range of cardboard boxes vary in sizes and shapes, so we can satisfy all customer needs.

What We Do?

Bespoke Manufacturing

We manufacture cardboard boxes to your exact size and specifications. From single, double or triple wall boxes to complex shelf ready packaging, we aim to offer you high quality products.

Our Commitment

Pearl Packaging is 360° Committed

Pearl Packaging is committed to delivering premium corrugated packaging solutions that protect products, serve industries, and sustain the environment. We strive for excellence in manufacturing, design, and customer service while maintaining the highest quality standards.
