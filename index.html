About Us

A Tradition of Excellence

Pearl Packaging Company is a corrugated packaging manufacturer based in Karachi, Pakistan. Established in 2006, the privately owned company provides a first class service to the packaging industry. The cardboard manufacturer is one of the biggest independent units in Karachi and continues to grow in the packaging field.

Along with its sister companies, Pearl Packaging Company delivers packaging solutions from design, to manufacturing and printing. We offer a comprehensive range of cardboard products, from a basic carton box to the most intricate and unique die-cut boxes.
