Sectors We Serve

Cross-Industry Expertise

Pearl Packaging provides corrugated solutions to a wide range of industries, from food & beverage to textiles, pharmaceuticals, and e-commerce.

Food & Beverage

Custom packaging for bakeries, confectioneries, beverages, and food processing companies including Pepsi, Coca-Cola, Candyland, and Slice.

Textiles & Apparel

Premium packaging solutions for fashion brands including Al-Karam, Lucky Textile, Amir Adnan, and Oaks.

Pharmaceuticals

Secure, regulation-compliant packaging for pharmaceutical companies like AGP.

FMCG

High-volume corrugated packaging for fast-moving consumer goods manufacturers including Danpak and Crown.

Electronics

Protective packaging with custom inserts for electronics and technology products.

E-Commerce

Shipping-ready corrugated boxes designed to protect products during transit.
